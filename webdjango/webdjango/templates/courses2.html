English Literature

Description: The "English Literature" course explores literary works written in English across various
time periods and by diverse authors. The course typically includes the study of poetry, plays, novels,
short stories, and non-fiction texts. It aims to develop students' critical and analytical understanding of
English literature and enhance their linguistic and expressive skills.

Prerequisites:

  • English A
  • English B
